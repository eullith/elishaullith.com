Who's elisha?

Hey there, I'm an experienced freelance eLearning Designer and Developer. I am all about crafting fun, interactive, and engaging eLearning courses in Articulate Storyline and Rise.

How

My journey as an eLearning designer and developer took flight during my pursuit of a software engineering diploma. It all began when I enrolled in a multimedia development class, which introduced me to the creation of eLearning content. This pivotal experience occurred around 2019 and sparked my passion for the field.

Why

Inspired by the potential of eLearning and its ability to revolutionize education, I made a conscious decision to venture into freelance eLearning development. This decision opened doors to incredible opportunities, as I began collaborating with talented individuals who not only guided me but also helped me nurture my skills and gain expertise in this dynamic and rapidly evolving field.
