Comparing Learning Theories

Theory Focus Learning Process Role of Learner Role of Teacher Example
Behaviourism Observable Behaviours Stimulus-Response Passive Receiver Active Instructor Teaching basic skills like riding a bike
Constructivism Constructing Knowledge Constructing Meaning Active Participant Facilitator, Guide Group projects, Inquiry-based learning
Cognitivism Mental Processes Information Processing Active Participant Facilitator, Guide, Mentor Mnemonics, Metaphors, Concept maps
Connectivism Networked Learning Navigating and Connecting Active Networked Participant Facilitator, Network Weaver Collaborative Learning, Social Media
Andragogy Adult Learning & Experience Self-Directed Learning Self-Directed, Experienced, Active Learner Facilitator, Resource Provider Mentoring, Personalized Learning
