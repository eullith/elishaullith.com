Week 7

Microlearning Project

Technology tool

I enjoy creating graphics and vectors in Adobe Illustrator for this specific course. I wanted to design and make all the visual media I would integrate into my portfolio. However, I am new to creating icons and using a specific style guide so all can be look-a-like.
I chose to use Adobe Illustrator for my project because:
  • It is a highly in-demand tool in the design industry
  • It is known for its versatility and flexibility in creating vector graphics
  • It is a great choice for producing high-quality artwork and designs
Additionally, I identified some gaps in my skills and knowledge with Adobe Illustrator. By working on this project, I hope to:
  • Improve my skills and knowledge with Adobe Illustrator
  • Gain more experience and confidence in using this tool
  • Produce a final product that showcases my newly acquired skills

Final Products Created With the Tool

Supporting Theories

Cognitivism: Observing experienced graphic designers using Illustrator helped me expand my knowledge of the software. I learned new techniques, gained confidence in my abilities, and developed my own style. It also helped me understand the design process and how to approach challenges creatively.
Connectivism: Having access to online tutorials and communities that helped me connect with experts that shared their knowledge and work on YouTube.
Constructivism: I was able to experiment with different methods and techniques to create my own designs, which helped me to develop my own style. Also, I was able to reflect on my own learning process and identify areas where I needed to improve.
Andragogy: I was able to take ownership of my own learning process and set my own goals and objectives for learning illustrator. Also, I was able to apply my new skills directly to my portfolio project, which made it meaningful and engaging.

My 'ah ha' moments

One of my biggest 'ah ha' moments in this microlearning project was discovering new techniques in Adobe Illustrator that were easy to apply and enriched my designs with a unique style. Before, I struggled to create designs that stood out and spent hours replicating designs without understanding underlying principles. However, these new techniques allowed me to create designs that reflected my own unique perspective and style, and added depth to my projects. Overall, this moment was a game-changer for me and reminded me of the joy of learning and exploring new things.
